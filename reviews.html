Movies

Moonlight(2016)

Dir. Barry Jenkins
Rating: 9/10

“Sometimes round the way, where we live, you can catch this same breeze.” Few have explored isolation and identity in such a poignant portrait as Barry Jenkins has in his 2016 film, “Moonlight.” I was so enchanted by the way Jenkins viscerally immerses us into Chiron’s world. Upon subsequent rewatchings, I felt especially drawn to the way Jenkins portrayed the ocean and its occurrences along Chiron’s journey to self-acceptance.


Steven Universe: Change Your Mind(2019)

Dir. Joe Johnston, Kat Morris
Rating: 8.5/10

An incredibly satisfying conclusion to the Steven Universe storyline. Themes: poignant. Subtext: powerful. Music: memorable.
